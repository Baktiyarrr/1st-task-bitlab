Khabib to Conor:"It is the same thing"

Khabib Nurmagomedov has given Conor McGregor a narrow window if he is to come out on top in next month’s trilogy fight against Dustin Poirier.

With the score standing at 1-1 in the rivalry after Poirier became the first man to TKO McGregor in mixed martial arts, the lightweight contenders will take to the Octagon for a third time in the main event of UFC 264 on July 10.

Nurmagomedov has previously backed protege Islam Makhachev to ultimately become the UFC’s 155lbs champion and he is impressed by the Russians currently plying their trade in the lightweight division, although he doubts that we will see an all-Russian title fight any time soon.
